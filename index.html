Agriculture takes up 70% of our daily water use and the largest demand for the world’s water comes from agriculture. However, it is very inefficient and has a low-value, yet it is one of the top water users. Almost 60% of the water diverted or pumped for irrigation is wasted. The wasted water seeps back into the ground causing waterlogging and salinity. Poor irrigation drainage can increase the amount of water related diseases which can result in human suffering and may even lead to death. As you can see, many problems occur due to the over usage of water for irrigation. This is why we need to do something to decrease the amount of water wasted for agriculture.
